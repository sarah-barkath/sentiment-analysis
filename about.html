About the Application

Sentiment analysis is a popular task in natural language processing. The goal of sentiment analysis is to classify the text based on the mood or mentality expressed in the text.


This model processes the twitter dataset of 124M tweets from Jan 2018 to Dec 2021 and implements the RoBERTa base model for training. It focuses on 3 major aspects of emotions: positivity, negativity and neutrality.
